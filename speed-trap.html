The Flash: Speed Trap

Preliminary Edit Overview

The Flash: Speed Trap is a two-part restructure. This part is built around rise of The Flaah. Wells is reimagined as a true mentor, while Barry's growth is streamlined through montages.

The romance is reduced to strengthen the main narrative, allowing the season’s emotional weight to center on Barry, Wells, and the Man in Yellow. The edit ends with Barry’s bested by Reverse Flash
