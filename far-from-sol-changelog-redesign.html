Access Change Log ⌄
GLOBAL CHANGES
  • Reconstructed the film into a mostly linear narrative structure.
  • Added a recurring family flashback storyline for Grace, providing additional emotional context and motivation.
  • Introduced a recurring leitmotif adapted from The Expanse's Docking at Lovell City OST, rearranged and reorchestrated throughout the film to musically connect Grace's memories, mission acceptance, and final decision.
  • Corrected anamorphic light tower effects so bloom remains properly contained behind the letterbox bars.
  • Added new Light Speed Studios logo.
OPENING & EARTH
  • Rebuilt opening around the discovery of the Petrova Line and Astrophage.
  • Expanded Astrophage montage with additional footage.
  • Added new FAR FROM SOL title card after opening montage.
  • Moved Grace's classroom sequence to the beginning of the film.
  • Added Family Flashback #1 following the classroom sequence, scored with the family leitmotif.
  • Removed Grace asking Carl if the world is really ending.
  • Reduced humor throughout the quarantine lab scenes.
  • Removed Stratt consulting with delegates before responding to Grace's concern about being a guinea pig.
  • Removed Grace's false discovery and "nothing happened" exchange.
  • Removed Grace asking delegates if they are excited.
  • Kept Grace and Carl's shopping spree.
  • Streamlined Grace's discovery of Astrophage reproduction by removing the exchange with Carl about sitting down and being a dad.
  • Now the scene transitions to Grace on phone with Stratt.
  • Retained Project Hail Mary briefing on the ship, Tau Ceti discovery, and mission planning sequences.
GRACE'S RECRUITMENT
  • Retained Grace meeting the astronaut recruits and Astrophage experiment.
  • Added Family Flashback #2 after Grace's discussion with Yao about bravery.
  • Scored with rearranged family leitmotif.
  • Replaced Grace's awkward exchange with couple with a warmer smile.
  • Retained Stratt's singing scene.
  • Retained Stratt/Grace mission briefing and explosion.
  • Reworked Grace's mission acceptance.
  • Removed the implication that Grace was forcibly recruited.
  • Removed Stratt's line telling Grace he has "no immediate family... not even a dog."
  • The new family storyline now develops through the recurring flashbacks instead.
  • Reframed Grace's acceptance of the mission as a reluctant yes shown through a new launch montage.
  • Added launch preparation montage featuring custom countdown and launch sequences.
  • Reused the family leitmotif during this montage
SHIP AWAKENING
  • Streamlined Grace's awakening by reducing the amnesia-driven "figuring out" sequence and removing many of its comedic beats.
  • Repurposed existing footage into a new montage of Grace sorting through his crewmates' belongings, and visiting the ships plaques, rescored with "From Gold" by Novo Amor.
  • Added Grace saying "I commend you to the stars" during the crew burial sequence, restoring a fan-favorite line from the novel.
  • Added brief family memory insert while Grace observes the virtual beach.
FIRST CONTACT
  • Removed extended ships "playing tag" sequence between Grace and Rocky.
  • Rocky sends the bottle message immediately upon establishing contact.
  • Removed Grace tripping after restoring gravity.
  • Retained all of first contact and communication-development scenes.
ROCKY ABOARD
  • Reduced recurring "dirty ship" jokes as Rocky explores Grace's ship.
  • Removed Rocky's request for Grace to demonstrate the sampling mission using ship figures and a puppet show.
  • Rest of interactions are mostly intact with minor tweaks.
  • Rescored the Tau Ceti E transit montage with Sugar Ray's "Someday" and restructured portions of the sequence to better align key visual beats with the song's emotional progression, further emphasizing Grace and Rocky's growing friendship.
FAMILY ARC
  • Added extended family confession sequence during Grace and Rocky's discussion of their mates.
  • Added Family Flashback #3.
  • Expanded use of the family leitmotif during Grace's confession to Rocky.
  • Introduced new ADR revealing Grace's family history and loss.
  • Restructured scenes and ADR to deepen Grace and Rocky's emotional bond.
  • Established new emotional foundation and motivations for Grace's later choices.
SCIENCE & DISCOVERY
  • Retained Grace's Astrophage collection sequence.
  • Rescored Astrophage analysis montage with "Circles" by Vanic (instrumental).
  • Retained all of sample collection scenes and biosphere investigation sequences.
ROCKY'S SACRIFICE
  • Removed "That's just something people say" line following Rocky's Astrophage gift.
  • Removed dialogue regarding when a hug ends.
  • Allowed the hug sequence to play without comedic interruption.
  • Retained the full biosphere sampling sequence.
  • Retained Grace's injury and Rocky's rescue.
  • Retained Taumoeba breakthrough and mission success sequences.
  • Retained Rocky's recovery sequence and Grace's visit to Rocky's ship.
NEW ENDING
  • Completely restructured Grace's final decision.
  • Removed Grace's departure storyline, fuel-loss subplot, and eventual return to Rocky.
  • Added Family Flashback #4 and new montage contrasting Grace's family memories with his experiences alongside Rocky.
  • Reframed Grace's decision to remain on Erid as an emotional choice.
  • Reworked probe-launch sequence to reflect Grace's new decision.
FINAL SCENES & END CREDITS
  • Reworked Rocky's final conversation with Grace to support the revised ending.
  • Removed the implication that Grace remains on Erid because of ship limitations.
  • Reframed Grace's choice to make Erid his home.
  • Removed the scenes showing Grace teaching Eridian children, allowing the film to conclude on the relationship between Grace and Rocky.
  • Added custom LSS end credit and FAR FROM SOL title card.
